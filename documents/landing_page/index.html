Human-in-the-Loop

Your messenger as a second factor

Every time an AI agent oversteps its boundaries, Konkin.io pauses and sends an approval request to your chosen messenger (Telegram, Discord, etc.). The agent waits.
You decide. No approval = no funds move.

  • 1 AI agent requests a wallet action via MCP
  • 2 Konkin.io queues the request & sends you a message
  • 3 You tap Approve or Deny — from anywhere
  • 4 Konkin.io acts and relays the decision back to the agent

Core Capabilities

A custom safeguard for your secrets!

AI agents are not to be trusted with critical secrets. KONKIN is a local MCP server that bridges AI agents with your self-hosted crypto wallet daemons — effectively removing direct access to your secrets from the agent.

🔒

Fully Self-Hosted

Runs entirely on your machine. No cloud, no custody, no trust — just you, your keys, and your wallet daemons.

👤

Human-in-the-Loop

Transactions are queued for your approval via Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, the Rest-Api, or a local web UI. The agent waits.

⚖️

Configurable Quorum

Set up 1-of-N approval (any channel approves), N-of-M multi-sig-style control, or custom auto-rules by amount, time, or volume.

📋

Audit Log

You decide what gets recorded. Full history of agent requests, approvals, and denials — stored locally and owned by you.

🧩

Modular Wallet Support

Bitcoin, Monero, and Litecoin today — with a growing list of coins added as dedicated wallet modules over time.

🔌

MCP Native

Your AI agent understands the coin protocol immediately, so you don't have to.
